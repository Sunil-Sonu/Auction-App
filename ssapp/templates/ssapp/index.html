What We Do

This is where you can find your desired products at an affordable Prices. All you have to do is bid an amount on the product which you want to buy and,

Search

Every Product is categorized in Categories so that it would easy for you to search for your desired product.

Interface

You will have two tabs. One, where all the products that you have bid on are displayed (MyBids) and other tab(Products Won) is where all the products that you won are displayed along with the owner's information so that you can contact him directly

Usage

All you have to do it, go the Categories, Bid for a product you want and if the seller likes your bid amount, he will click on the sell item and then the product is added to your (Products Won) tab with all the details of the seller so that you can contact him directly.
